Divine Drip's History

Humble Beginnings

Two decades ago, Harold MacDonald, the company's founder, was poor and had a limited amount of clothes to wear. One day, while meditating, he visualized an angel descending from the sky and giving him gold & white clothes. Shocked, Harold stopped meditating, briefly pondered what he saw, & went to sleep. The next morning, when Harold opened his front door after hearing someone knock, he saw radiant, elegant, & gold and white clothes on the ground.

A Herald's Requiem

Shortly after receiving the clothes from an unknown source, Harold decided to start a clothing company to help others feel as otherwordly as he did when he wore them. A few years later, he built an affluent business that's rapidly growing in popularity.

Milestones

Divine Drip has achieved outstanding milestones since it was founded:

  • Fortune 500 Company
  • Earned a net value of $500 million in 2020
  • Garnered massive clout since its inception
  • Just being a cool company ;)
